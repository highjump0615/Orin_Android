Which devices are currently supported?

We currently support all iOS devices version 4+ (iPad, iPhone and iPod Touch).

Are Kindle Fire devices supported?

The app won't work on Kindle Fire devices but we intend to develop for it in the future. Therefore, we don't distribute the app via Amazon's Appstore for Android. Instead, you can download the app below.

The app does not seem to work.

Please check the following steps to troubleshoot your Orin Mobile app:

Is your internet connection active?

Have you entered the correct login and password?

The app crashes on start

Try restarting your device and open the app again. If it continues to crash, you can e-mail us at hello@orin.io

Why are there no badges displayed on my device?

Badges aren't displayed if you use iOS 3 or earlier. Please update your device to the latest iOS. If you still don't see any badges in Orin Mobile you can email the developer at hello@orin.io.

What if I lose my phone?

Orin Mobile doesn't store your password on the device. You account only requires view access.

How do I report a suggestion or a bug in the Mobile app?

You can e-mail us at hello@orin.io
